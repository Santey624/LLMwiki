## NepMedJP — Cross-Lingual Medical Summarization
**Role:** Lead Researcher
**Period:** 2025 – Present

A research initiative targeting Japanese–Nepali cross-lingual medical text summarization for low-resource healthcare communication.

### Work
- Establishing the first benchmark dataset and evaluation framework for Japanese–Nepali medical summarization (source: santoshCV (1).pdf)
- Performed continued pretraining and fine-tuning across multiple hyperparameters (source: santoshCV (1).pdf)
- Dataset construction and validation in collaboration with three Nepalese medical doctors (source: santoshCV (1).pdf)
- Targeting submission to top NLP conferences (source: santoshCV (1).pdf)

### Stack
Python, PyTorch, mT5, Transformers, Cross-Lingual NLP, Medical NLP, SFT, Docker, Linux, GPU Cluster
